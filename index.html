About Annual Charaty Show (ASC)

ASC is a Nepal registered charity dedicated to creating a better future for animals in need. Our first project, established in 2009, is based in Sabah, Borneo, where we have a facility and team providing a lifeline to thousands of dogs and cats every year in need of veterinary care and support. Our companion animal programmes have since developed in other Countries, including Romania, Penang, Philippines and the UK.
